Our Services

Diary plants & Equipments

Enriched with vast industrial experience, we are able to be supplying extensive range of Dairy Plants and Equipment. Our offered plants and equipment are widely used in various dairy plants for storing milk and transporting it from plant to the place of final destination. Configured with high quality features, these products are known for their easy operations and flawless performance. Furthermore, customers can avail these products at reasonable rates.

Horizontal Milk Storage Tanks

Designed and manufactured to perfection, we are one of the most excellent and leading manufacturers and suppliers of Horizontal Milk Storage Tank. This range is manufactured using superior quality stainless steel and is widely used for offering processing solutions to the diary industry.

Butter Milk Tanks

Leveraging on our wide experience and knowledge in this domain, our company is considered as one of the well equipped manufacturers and suppliers of Butter Milk Tank.

Conveyor Systems

We develop quality certified Conveyors that are applicable in various manufacturing industries, such as food & beverages, dairy plants etc. We design these conveyors using finest material that is corrosion free and can bear heavy weight. These are offered in various lengths and capacities as per the requirements of our clients. These are noiseless and easily rotate without any discrepancies. We use latest method of productions, which make the process of loading & unloading, packaging and movement in these conveyors smooth and hassle free.

Milk Chilling Plants

A range of big size milk chilling plants is supplied by us, which can cool the liquid in a bulk. These plants are equipped with the both direct expansion cooling tanks and ice bank tanks. These are of 1000 ltr to 10,000 Ltr capacity per day.

Instant Cooling Plants

We are able to supplying a comprehensive range of Instant Cooling Plant. The offered machine is manufactured at par with international quality standards & norms, using premium grade components and sophisticated technology. With our expertise in this domain, we are able to offer a superior quality range of machine. We tend to deliver these machines to our clients within the stipulated time frame.

Milk Silo

We are offerings Milk Silo that is installed in dairy plants for bulk storage of milk. We construct these silos by using high quality stainless steel, which is corrosion free, highly robust and can withstand tough weather conditions. The milk silo is installed outside and close to dairy plant. We offer silo in various capacities as to meet the demands of our clients. These are precisely constructed with cleaning system that ensures pure and clean quality of milk. We provide silo tanks with cooling jackets that help in maintaining the milk temperature.

Milk Chillers

We are offering technologically advanced range of Milk Chillers that is developed from high grade stainless steel, which can withstand extreme cold temperature. In this chiller, raw milk is rapidly cooled to low temperature in order to inhibit the growth of the micro-organism present in milk. This helps in increasing the consumption life of milk and keeps it fresh. Inside the chiller, fat in milk does not churn due to rough handling or excessive agitation.

Batch Pasteurizer

We are engaged in offering supreme quality Batch Pasteurizer, which is a tank with peripheral heating. This is installed in several dairies and food industries. We construct this batch pasteurizer using high quality stainless steel. The unit is completely rust proof, robust in design and keeps the dairy product contaminant free. It is used for heating the milk at a lower temperature for longer time, which is better for yogurt and soft cheese process.

C.I.P Systems

We provide C.I.P System that helps in maintaining the hygienic standards and protecting the products from degradation & contamination. This cleaning-in-place system has a pipeline that can be circulated or sprayed with a minimum of hand detailing and physical changes to the equipment configuration. The C.I.P system includes solution reservoir, supply pump, spray device and return circuit in the case of a tank of other similar vessel.. CIP (Cleaning-in-place) System is an automatic machine that is designed to clean and disinfect various processing plants. This reliable system is available in manual and automatic option and also meets the stringent hygiene regulations demanded by the food, dairy, pharmaceutical and biological industries. Furthermore, it also ensures food safety & highest sanitary standards in any processing plant.

Milk Cold Storage Room

Being a quality driven organization, we are actively committed towards offering well constructed Cold Storage Room. The offered range is precisely designed and construed using high grade components and latest technology. We assure our clients that our range of rooms is quality tested against predefined quality parameters, in order to meet international standards. Apart from this, our esteemed clients can avail the entire range, as per their need at reasonable price.

Ice Cream Plants

We develop quality approved Ice Cream Plant that is constructed using high grade stainless steel and raw materials. Our plant is built with innovative concept and by using latest machineries. Our plant produces have the advanced functions of preparing different flavours of ice creams. These are installed in various dairy industries and food industries. The ice cream processing includes boiling of milk, cooling, chilling of milk, mixing of milk, sugar, flavours and other ingredient into semi- solid form and then freezing, hardening and packaging..

Ice Bank Tanks

We are instrumental in providing highly efficient Ice Bank Tank. The offered product-range is manufactured by our highly skilled professionals by utilizing premium quality raw material in adherence to international quality standards.
