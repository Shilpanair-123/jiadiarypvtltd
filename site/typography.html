  • How technology can help save Nations groundwater ?

    About 50% of drinking water is globally provided by Groundwater. Almost 40% of water sources are used for agricultural purpose and 30% for industry. In order to sustain aquatic ecosystems, all have to maintain river baseflows. As part of strange global changes, there is a need to increase our understanding of groundwater.So that implementing conjunctive and adaptive management practices is the only way .Without proper knowledge about ground water its protection is impossible. Groundwater modelling is an important tool to estimate the catchment areas of the wells . Modelling can be used to provide information for optimized Groundwater protection and can be used in the planning of sustainable groundwater abstraction.

  • Groundwater Management Technology for Water Resource Management:

    Due to variations in environment and over exploitation of resources, the flow of groundwater has decreased. Gradually groundwater level has been reduced in the most parts of the country. This has led to severe water deficiency problems all over the country over the years. This can be solved by different direct and indirect methods of groundwater recharge technology. This technology can reduce the wastage of water and enhanced groundwater availability.

  • Conclusion

    All these technologies plays an important role in the reduction of surface runoff, increase availability of water for irrigation, domestic and industrial sector, improve the drainage, revival of springs and improvement of groundwater quality and so on. It is also considered to mitigate the impacts of variability in rainfall patterns under varying climatic conditions. Moreover it is primarily important to meet the demand of spatial water productivity and availability.

  • Everything You Need To Know about drilling Industries

    Water well drilling is the process of drilling holes in the earth's surface for the purpose of reaching a groundwater source. There are several water sources beneath the earth's surface but are difficult to reach and in some cases very deep. There are several drilling techniques that can be used for the purpose of drilling water wells and the most commonly used are rotary drillers that bore vertically into the earth. A well drilling rig is a large piece of equipment that must be brought to a site by truck and put into place on the site where the well is to be dug. The goal of well drilling is to reach an aquifer or water source deep below the ground, but that is not where the drilling stops. To ensure the well will have ample supply and a long life, the well drilling company will continue to drill below the water table.
